Aesthetic How will the commission look???

Typically when I draw, I like to use a Binary Pen, which produces a solid, kind of pixelated outline. I like the look of it personally, and I find that it generally makes life easier when it comes to the colouring stage.

I do sometimes use more conventional brushes if that is your preference, though that may affect the price depending on the level of detail.
The resolution of the commission will roughly be in the 2000x2000 range maximum, depending on what you're wanting.
