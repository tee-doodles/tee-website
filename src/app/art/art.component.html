Payment How will you steal my money from me???

I use PayPal for commissions, I don't have any other alternatives and don't plan on using any either.
Payment will be up-front in full, though this can be negotiated (if you'd prefer to see a sketch first or something)

Lineart How will the commission look???

Typically when I draw, I like to use a Binary Pen, which produces a solid, kind of pixelated outline. I like the look of it personally, and I find that it generally makes life easier when it comes to the colouring stage.

I do sometimes use more conventional brushes if that is your preference, though that may affect the price depending on the level of detail.
The resolution of the commission will roughly be in the 2000x2000 range maximum, depending on what you're wanting.

Tech What do you draw in???

I use Clip Studio Paint to draw :)
If you want the source files when I'm done with a commission, go ahead and ask and I'll send it to you.

References Can I show you what I want it to look like???

Of course! Using visual references for a commission, be it a picture of a pose, or a stickman drawing, or whatever, really helps out as it gives me a clearer idea of what you want. Just don't send me NSFW material, I'm not interested in seeing it.

Corrections & Adjustments Will you make changes for me???

If I've made some dumb error and it needs fixing (i.e missing bit of shading, or it's not blended), I'll fix that just fine :)
Regarding pretty big adjustments, for example if you want the pose changed, then I'll need to know BEFORE I start outlining onwards.
I'll send you the sketch when it's done and then wait for your approval to go ahead with outlining, after this point I'm going to assume it's all good and won't be changed.
